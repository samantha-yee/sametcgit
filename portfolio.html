etcetera magazine: gritzmore edition
Over the Summer of 2017, a group of friends started filming a sketch comedy series comprised of 3 ten-minute long episodes. Apart from contributing graphics for the episodes and print material for the series, I worked alongside the Gritzmore team to organize photoshoots and interviews for an ePublication.

Gritzmore has given many individuals a place to be creative and experimental with story telling. I am extremely grateful to have a group of friends I can share this creative outlet with.
