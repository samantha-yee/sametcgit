I’ve never been a big fan of breakfast and I’ve always thought of lunch as just a necessary meal to keep yourself going throughout the day. But put them together and take me out for brunch any day of the week! Oh how I love the richness of hollandaise over my eggs bene-dict or the mixture of sweet and savoury you get out of chicken and waffles. Brunch lets me indulge without guilt. It’s that wonderful meal we’ve created for us lovers of food but not lovers of breakfast.
