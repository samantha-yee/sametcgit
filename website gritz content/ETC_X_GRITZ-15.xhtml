What would a dad sketch show be in your opinion?

I think with dad sketch shows and our parents’ era of comedy, everything just goes on for so long. It’s like, current stuff really knows when to cut and when people get the punchline. My dad really loves Key and Peele. And this may be a hot take, but I’m not a big fan. Comedy for dads goes on for too long and is too much about stuff that’s dated. But that’s what I think. Stuff that’s like, ‘Oh that used to be funny.’ Relevant isn’t the right word.

Maybe this is just because we’re the age that we are, but comedic timing and ridicu-lousness is also more popular. I don’t want to say it’s a trend, but I guess we are used to the sitcom stuff growing up and now we feel like it’s dated.

Yea, definitely. We’re striving to be like, ‘Whoa— where did that come from?’ Rather than, ‘Don’t you notice, everybody loves avocado on their toast?’ Stuff like that. ‘Look at all these hip kids with their expensive coffee.’
