“My life keeps imitating my art--

a very scary thing.”

David Greene is a young filmmaker living in Vancouver, BC. His first season of the sketch comedy series, Gritzmore, takes what life throws at you and puts it in a world where the absurd is the norm. There’s no such thing as taking offence in this reality, giving us a place to make light of what we’ve made heavy in our real world. It has become a collaborative project that is making David and the team behind Gritzmore’s visions come to life on screen. It’s work, play, a labour of love, another reason to be among friends, and is a beautiful example of what a group of millennials can do together.
